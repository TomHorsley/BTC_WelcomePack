Microchips & Registration

Your dog will be microchipped before leaving here, and this will be registered to us (if you are foster family/back-up please ask your rescuer for the guidelines on fostering).

Microchip transfer information

To start the registration process contact 'Animal Tracker / Identibase' by logging onto the pet microchip database or call them on the number provided below:

Animal Tracker:

01279 219777

animaltracker.co.uk

You will be asked to pay an annual fee of only £15 (at the time of writing) which can be done via their website or by telephone call. They will then contact me with your registration request which I will authorise.

For E.U.Adoptions you must comply with the dog laws set out by your country of residence.

Animal Tracker offer all kinds of benefits and services to their customers especially those that have chosen a rescue, such as a lifetime of as many updates to your registration as you like, for example, a change of telephone number/address and they also do engraved identity tags at only £9.99 for two - price correct at time of writing. Be sure to mention that you adopted from Break the Chains for Dogs Bulgaria and ask them about their varying offers.
